Drive-Awake 

is an embedded system integrated in vehicules provided with a web platform and a mobile application.

Our embedded system integrated in the steering wheel contains three sensors for heart-rate,temperature and alcohol detection in order to monitor the health state of the driver so he/she can drive safely on the road

The camera implemented in the car detects the drowziness and tiredness of the driver through eye-blinking

In case of an alert detection the system will activate the led and buzzer on the dashboard and it will also display an alert message also the gsm and gps modules integrated in the cars will get the location of the car and send an alert message to the emergency services containing the location of the driver.

The mobile application Drive-Awake allows the driver to display the physiological parameters also it contains an awarness interface where he can check on the informations needed to aware him about how to prevent for car accidents and take care of his health. In case of an alert detected the mobile application will send an SOS message with the location of the driver to a number that he will set on the app.
